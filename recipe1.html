A delicacy of pilau served with kachumbari and chutney

Ingredients

  • Rice of choice:Basmati 2 cups
  • Protein of choice:Goat 500g cubed
  • Onions-2large thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves,mincedGarlic and 1 table spoon ,gratedGinger
  • Pilau masala 1-2tsp
  • Water-4 cups
  • cooking oil-3-4 tablespoons
  • Cumin seeds 1 spoon
  • Cinnamon stick-1
  • Bay leaves 1-2
  • Black peppercorns 1tsp

Instructions

  1. Prep the goat meat
  2. Fry the onions
  3. add spieces
  4. Add garlic& ginger
  5. Add tomatoes
  6. Add beef
  7. Add the rice
  8. Add broth
  9. Simmer for about 20 minutes after you cover it tightly,one can cover it using aluminium foil to keep it moist
  10. Let it rest for about 5-10 minutes afterwards you can fluff the rice with the fork
