RICE CHICKEN

A delicacy of rice chicken served with plain yourghut

Ingredients

  • Chicken-boneless 500g cubed
  • Long grain basmati rice-2 cups
  • a pinch of salt
  • 500g-1kg pork
  • 1-2 onions slicely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Garlic 3-4 cloves,minced
  • Chicken broth-4 cups
  • 1tsp of curry powder
  • Chilli powder to taste
  • Cinnamon sticks

Instructions

  1. prep the chicken while seasoning it lightly with salt,ginger,black pepper and salt then marinate it
  2. Cook the chicken
  3. Add my spices
  4. Add broth and let it cook for about 15 minutes
  5. Add the rice and stir it gently
  6. Cook the rice and cover it with the aluminium foil for about 20 minutes
  7. Turn off the heat and let it rest for about 5 minutes.Fluff the rice with a fork and serve it hot.
